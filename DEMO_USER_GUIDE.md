# TalentIQ AI - Beta Tester Demo Guide

Welcome to the TalentIQ-AI Beta preview! This guide walks you through the core product flow to evaluate the end-to-end user experience.

---

## 1. Sign Up & Create an Account
1. Launch the application and click **Sign In** (or navigate to `/auth`).
2. Click **Create an account** at the bottom of the card.
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Role (Student or Job Seeker)
4. Click **Register** to submit. You will be automatically redirected to your fresh **Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Navigate the Empty State & Upload Resume
1. Upon logging in for the first time, you will see a personalized greeting and a **Welcome Empty State** card.
2. Click the **Choose File** button inside the welcome box (or in the Quick Actions panel).
3. Select your text or PDF resume file and click **Upload Resume**.
4. A parsing stages indicator will appear, showcasing extraction steps (Reading file, normalizer pipeline, computing matching engines).

---

## 3. Review the Dashboard
Once parsing finishes, the dashboard dynamically populates:
- **Career & Resume Scores**: Located in the top cards. Hover over the information `(i)` icons next to the titles to read the exact calculation weights.
- **AI Insights & Recommendations**: Located in the center and sidebar panels, detailing key growth suggestions.
- **Skill Gaps**: Lists technical and soft skill gaps compared to your target industry.
- **Target Timeline**: Chronological events log mapping your career advancement.

---

## 4. Use TalentCoach (AI Mentor)
1. Click **TalentCoach** in the top navigation bar.
2. In the workspace text area, ask your mentor questions:
   - *Example*: "How can I close my React and Docker skill gaps?" or "Tell me about my resume achievements."
3. Click **Send to TalentCoach** and read the tailored feedback.
4. Accept recommended growth missions to add them to your daily goals.

---

## 5. Send Beta Feedback
Help us improve by sharing layout, performance, or AI bugs:
1. Click the floating **Beta Feedback** button in the bottom-right corner.
2. Select a rating (1 to 5 stars).
3. Describe any bugs, interface errors, or ideas in the text box.
4. Click **Submit Feedback**.
